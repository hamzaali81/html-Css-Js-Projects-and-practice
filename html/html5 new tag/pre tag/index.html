Writing An Application For Leave of Absence.

Suppose, your name is Richard Philip. You are a student of Leigh High School, San Jose. You could not attend school for five days on account of illness. Now, write an application to the Headmaster of your school for leave of absence.

22 April, 2016

The Headmaster

Leigh High School

San Jose

Subject: Application for leave of absence.

Sir,

With due respect I would like to inform you that I could not attend school from the 10th to the 15th instant on account of my illness.

In the circumstances, I hope that you would be kind enough to permit me to grant me leave of absence for those days.

Yours obediently

Richard Philip

Class - Eighth

Roll no. - 05
